New Workshop on Computational Statistics

I recently did an online workshop with District Data Labs on Computational Statistics methods. This course is a survey of contemporary methods in statistical computing, with a focus on Monte Carlo, Bootstrapping, Bayesian Inference, and MCMC.

The course videos have been made available here, and the supplementary course materials are available in this github repository.
